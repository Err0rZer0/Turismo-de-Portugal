Navigating the Nation

From the iconic yellow trams of Lisbon to the high-speed Alfa Pendular trains, discovering Portugal is effortless and scenic.

Rail (Comboios de Portugal)

The CP rail network connects all major cities. The high-speed Alfa Pendular is the fastest way to travel between Lisbon and Porto.

Metro Systems

Lisbon and Porto feature modern, efficient, and art-filled underground networks that cover most tourist hotspots.

Traditional Trams

No trip to Lisbon is complete without a ride on the Remodelado trams, especially the famous Route 28 through Alfama.

Car Rentals

Ideal for exploring the hidden beaches of the Alentejo or the mountain villages of Central Portugal where trains don't reach.
