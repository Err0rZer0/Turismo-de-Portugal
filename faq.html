Essential Traveler FAQs

Quick answers to common questions for a smooth and enjoyable Portuguese adventure.

What is the local currency?

Portugal uses the Euro (€). Credit cards are widely accepted, but it's helpful to have small cash for local cafes or markets.

Do people speak English?

English is widely spoken in major cities and tourist areas. In rural villages, older residents may only speak Portuguese, but they are incredibly helpful.

Is tipping expected?

Tipping is not mandatory. In restaurants, rounding up or leaving 5-10% for exceptional service is appreciated but not required.

What are the emergency numbers?

The general emergency number for police, fire, or medical assistance is 112.

When is the best time to visit?

Spring (April–June) and Autumn (September–October) offer the best weather and fewer crowds. Summer is great for beaches but can be very hot inland.
