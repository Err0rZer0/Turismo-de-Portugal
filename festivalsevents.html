A Nation in Celebration

Join the party and feel the heartbeat of Portugal. From the vibrant, sardine-scented street parties of the Santos Populares to world-renowned international music festivals, there is always a reason to celebrate.

Santos Populares

Celebrate Lisbon's patron saints with street parties, grilled sardines, and traditional music throughout June.

Wine Harvest Festivals

Join grape-stomping traditions and harvest celebrations in wine regions across Portugal each autumn.

Music Festivals

Experience world-class music festivals from rock and electronic to jazz and classical performances.

Medieval Fairs

Step back in time at authentic medieval markets with period costumes, crafts, and traditional entertainment.

Religious Processions

Witness centuries-old religious traditions with elaborate processions during Holy Week and local patron saint days.

Cultural Celebrations

Immerse yourself in regional festivals celebrating local traditions, folklore, and gastronomic specialties.
