The Wild Frontiers

Step into nature's pristine sanctuaries. Trek through the granite peaks of Peneda-Gerês National Park, explore the prehistoric laurel forests of Madeira, and connect with the raw, untamed beauty of the Iberian wilderness.

Peneda-Gerês National Park

Portugal's only national park features pristine wilderness, ancient villages, and diverse wildlife habitats.

Serra da Estrela

Explore Portugal's highest mountain range with glacial valleys, alpine meadows, and traditional shepherd culture.

Ria Formosa Natural Park

Discover a unique lagoon ecosystem with barrier islands, salt marshes, and exceptional birdwatching opportunities.

Arrábida Natural Park

Experience Mediterranean landscapes with limestone cliffs, secluded beaches, and aromatic scrubland.

Montesinho Natural Park

Wander through oak and chestnut forests in this remote northern park rich in biodiversity and tradition.

Sintra-Cascais Natural Park

Explore dramatic coastal cliffs, romantic palaces, and lush forests just minutes from Lisbon.
