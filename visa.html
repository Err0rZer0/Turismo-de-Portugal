Travel Requirements

Everything you need to know about documentation, customs, and entry protocols before you depart for Portugal.

Schengen Area Travelers

Citizens of the EU and Schengen member states do not require a visa. A valid National ID card or Passport is sufficient for entry.

Short-Stay (90 Days)

Visitors from countries like the USA, Canada, and Australia can enter for tourism for up to 90 days within a 180-day period without a visa.

Visa Requirements

If you are from a country outside the visa-exempt list, you must apply for a Schengen Visa (Type C) at your local Portuguese consulate.

Mandatory Documentation

  • Passport valid for at least 3 months beyond your stay
  • Proof of sufficient financial means
  • Travel health insurance (minimum €30,000 coverage)
  • Proof of accommodation or invitation letter
